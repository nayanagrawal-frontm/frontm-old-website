The Future of Shipping

Discover how FrontM’s solutions foster better collaboration, productivity, wellbeing and entertainment and lead to unprecedented operational efficiency.

13 Feb 2020·12 pages

The maritime industry has been thrust into a technological reckoning by the pandemic, revealing an urgent need for technology adoption that would have otherwise evolved gradually.

Despite this urgency, the maritime sector lags in embracing current and emerging technologies, falling behind global trends. This delay inhibits the industry from reaping the financial and time-saving rewards that technology can offer.

FrontM is spearheading progress in the maritime realm with a primary focus on enhancing crew productivity, human capital management and fostering sustainability. This whitepaper explores how FrontM’s solutions lead to better collaboration, productivity, wellbeing and entertainment and bring unprecedented levels of efficiency to shipping operations.

Key Takeaways

  • What kind of maritime technologies can unlock unprecedented operational efficiency?
  • How can crew managers ensure swift decision-making and enhance operational fluidity with emerging technology?
  • Using a 360° crew engagement platform like FrontM, crew managers can solve everything from hiring talent to increasing productivity and ensuring crew welfare.
